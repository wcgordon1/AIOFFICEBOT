## How ATS Evaluates Cover Letters

### Beginner Explanation

ATS systems scan for:
- Job title references  
- Skill alignment  
- Keyword match  
- Structure  
- Length  
- Formatting issues  

If your letter is one giant paragraph with no structure, it becomes hard to parse.

### Technical Depth

Cover letters are often indexed alongside resumes in candidate databases. Keyword weighting may influence searchability later. If you omit core technical terms found in the job description, your profile becomes less retrievable in recruiter searches.

Before submitting, run your draft through the [PopResume Cover Letter Analysis Tool](https://www.popresume.com/free-cover-letter-checker).

## Real World Example

### Weak Version

“I am very excited to apply for this position. I believe my skills would be a great fit.”

Why it fails:
- No role specificity  
- No skill reference  
- No measurable proof  

### Strong Version

“I am applying for the Backend Engineer role at Acme Corp. In my current position, I built and maintained Node.js APIs serving 120k monthly users and reduced database query time by 37 percent through schema optimization.”

Why it works:
- Exact job title mentioned  
- Metrics included  
- Technology specified  
- Immediate relevance
